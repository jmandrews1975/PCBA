Who We Are

The bar for Pueblo County

The Pueblo County Bar Association has served the legal community of southeastern Colorado for over a century. We bring together attorneys, judges, and legal professionals to strengthen the practice of law and expand access to justice for all residents.

We are a voluntary bar association — a professional home for Pueblo's legal community, not a licensing body. Our work spans professional development, community outreach, pro bono service, and advocacy for a fair and accessible court system.

Access to JusticeFree clinics and referrals for residents who need legal help but can't afford it.
Professional DevelopmentCLE courses and networking opportunities that keep Pueblo attorneys sharp.
Community LeadershipAdvocating for a well-functioning, independent judiciary in Pueblo County.
